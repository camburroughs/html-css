Eggs Bites

Description

This recipe is an easy to make and easy to re-heat breakfast idea for those on the run. This recipe can easily be adjusted to fit your macro and nutritional needs.

*This recipe is made for a 6 bun-pit muffin tin.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup + 2 Tbsp liquid egg whites
  • 6 whole eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 slices of deli ham
  • 1 cup raw spinach
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Spray muffin tin generously with cooking spray
  3. To each bun-pit add 3 Tbsp of liquid egg whites and 1 whole egg. Scramble with a fork.
  4. Rip up the slices of deli ham to small pieces and evenly distribute between the un-pits.
  5. Evenly divide the spinach amoung the bun-pits.
  6. Gently mix each bun-pit to distribute ingredients
  7. Top each bun-pit with a sprinkle of cheddar cheese and place in middle rack of oven.
  8. After 20 min, or when the eggs are fully set, pull out the egg bites and let cool. You'll know they are ready when they easy pull apart from the tin.
  9. Store in tupperware or wrap in plastic wrap. To reheat, place on a plate and microwave for 30sec-1min.
